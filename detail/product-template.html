Frozen French Fries Production Line

This automated production line is designed for high-efficiency and mass production of frozen french fries. Made with food-grade SUS304 stainless steel, it ensures food safety and easy cleaning. The system covers everything from raw potato lifting, peeling, slicing, blanching, drying, frying, to final freezing and packaging.

Technical Specifications

Capacity 50 - 5000 KG/H (Customizable)
Material SUS 304 Stainless Steel
Power Source Electric / Gas / Steam (Optional)
Application Potatoes, Sweet Potatoes
