Planetary Stirring Cooking Mixer

An advanced automatic cooking mixer designed for high-viscosity foods. The stirrer features a planetary motion (rotating while revolving) that perfectly scrapes the hemispherical bottom of the wok, completely eliminating blind spots and preventing ingredients from sticking or burning. Ideal for sauces, caramel, jams, and curries.

Technical Specifications

ApplicationHot sauce, jam, caramel, lotus paste, central kitchen cooking
Stirring MethodPlanetary motion with Teflon scrapers
Heating SourceGas / Electromagnetic (Induction) / Steam / Thermal Oil
AutomationHydraulic tilting for easy discharge and cleaning
