Skip Car Cleaning Machine

Specifically designed for the meat and food processing industry, this compact device automatically cleans 200L standard material carts (skip cars/meat buggies). It automatically lifts, inverts, and thoroughly washes the interior and exterior using a high-pressure hot water rotary spray system, saving immense manual labor and ensuring hygiene compliance.

Technical Specifications

CapacityApproximately 20-30 skip cars per hour
Compatible SizeStandard 200L meat buggies (DIN 9797)
Cleaning MethodHigh-pressure spray (Hot water + Clean water rinse)
MaterialFull SUS 304 Stainless Steel construction
